Pricing

Notary Public Fees in California

The following are the maximum fees that a notary public can charge for their services in California:

Service Fee
Acknowledgments $15 per signature
Jurats $15 per signature
Oaths or Affirmations $15 per person
Certified Copies of Power of Attorney $15 per copy
Deposition Services
  • Administering the oath: $7
  • Certificate for a deposition: $7
  • Transcribing depositions: Actual cost, varies

Please note: Additional fees may be charged for travel, which are not regulated by the state and can vary based on the notary's discretion and distance traveled. Always confirm the fees with the notary beforehand.
